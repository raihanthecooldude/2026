The [GRaM](https://gram-workshop.github.io/) workshop aims to provide a platform that fosters learning, collaboration, and the advancement of the geometry-grounded methods in machine learning. In this Blogpost track, we intend to encourage transparent discussions and opinions in the field, and make geometric machine learning more accessible.

This blogpost track is directly inspired by the amazing [ICLR blogpost track](https://iclr-blogposts.github.io).

**Authors are requested to deanonymize their posts, include affiliations, and submit any final revisions by April 20, 2026.**
